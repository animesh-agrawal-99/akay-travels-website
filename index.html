Our Story

A Banarasi family that knows every lane of the old city.

Akay Tours & Travels grew up alongside Varanasi itself. What began as a small travel desk near Nadesar has, over three decades, become a trusted name for pilgrims arriving for Kashi Vishwanath darshan, families touring the ghats, and visitors crossing into Sarnath, Ayodhya and Prayagraj.

We do the planning that a guidebook cannot: a boatman who reaches the ghat before dawn, a driver who knows which gate to the temple is open, and an itinerary paced for both devotion and rest. Domestic or international, group or family, every journey is arranged with the same care.

With you on every step of the journey, Anil Agrawal Founder · Akay Tours & Travels

काशी दर्शन · Kashi Darshan

The experiences that define a journey to Varanasi.

We arrange each of these as a standalone visit or weave them into a full darshan itinerary.

01

Kashi Vishwanath

Darshan at the Jyotirlinga, with help navigating queues, timings and the Vishwanath Corridor.

02

Ganga Aarti

Reserved viewing for the evening aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, by ghat seat or by boat.

03

Sunrise Boat Ride

A quiet morning row along the ghats as the city wakes, the most photographed view of Kashi.

04

Sarnath

A half-day to where the Buddha gave his first sermon, the Dhamek Stupa and museum.
